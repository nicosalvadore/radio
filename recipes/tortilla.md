# Tortilla / Pita

**4 tortillas · 30 min**

## Ingredients

- 200g all-purpose flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 120ml water

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and form a dough ball.
2. Let it rest at least 15 min.
3. Split into 4 equal dough balls.
4. Preheat a skillet on high.
5. Flatten the first ball and cook on each side.
6. Repeat with the remaining dough balls.
