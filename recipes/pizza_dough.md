# Pizza Dough

**4 pizzas · 48h**

## Ingredients

- 640g farina 00
- 4dl water
- 20g salt
- 1/3 packet dry yeast (approximately)
- 8g sugar
- 20g olive oil

## Instructions

**48h before cooking:**

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
2. Work the dough by hand for a few minutes until it becomes too sticky to knead.
3. Let it rest 30 min to 1 hour.
4. Stretch and fold a few times.
5. Let it rest 1 hour.
6. Stretch and fold a few times again.
7. Weigh and split the dough into 4 equal balls.
8. Place each ball in a separate airtight container with a little semolina at the bottom and on top of the dough.
9. Store in the fridge for two nights.

**Day of cooking (around 14h):**

10. Remove the containers from the fridge, open them and leave the lids loosely on top.
11. Cover with a towel to keep out the light. The dough will rise during the afternoon as it comes to room temperature.
12. In the evening, preheat the pizza oven and cook as usual.
